Contoso Power BI Project

Dashboard report that analyse the sales from Contoso in the last years

This dashboard was created using Power Bi, after downloading Contoso database that contains all the information from the company related to the years 2007,2008, and 2009. I decided to focus on the sales from this company, and the profit that they gained in each of the operation years.

After setting up all the columns and tables I will need for this project, I started writing some key measures that will help me making calculations and get accurate results in each chart and table that is displayed in the dashboard.

In this dashboard you can find the total amount of sales, profit, average profit, a chart that displays the profit by year, bar chart that shows profit by quarter in each year, it is also displayed a descomposition tree with the profit and sales of each product from each channel that the store has, at the same time at the bottom there is 2 tables that show the top 10 and the worst 10 selling products from the store, there is also a pie chart that shows the profit from each channel of distribution.z
