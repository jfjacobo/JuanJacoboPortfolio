WMBA Web App

This application tracks baseball statistics, teams, players, and divisions. Built with C#, ASP.NET, MVC, Bootstrap, SQL, and JavaScript.

In this group project, we worked with WMBA to develop a baseball stats tracking application, I worked in the back-end, focusing on the MVC design pattern, developing the models, controllers, and database context that will be fundamental in the app functionality.

I also worked as the paperwork manager, organizing and recording all the documents that are part of the project such as the work breakdown structure, AI usage, bug list, feedback implementation, and 2 files that include all the planning stage for the project development and every step we follow to achieve the prototype objectives.
